THE REALITY OF SHARIA: A SYSTEM OF TOTAL CONTROL

Raymond Ibrahim--source on YouTube: "If Islam is the doctrinal blueprint of the faith, Sharia Law is the mandatory legal engine that enforces and upholds it in the physical world."

I. The Penal Code: The Silent Law Within the Ummah

Strict Sharia operates under fixed penalties (Hudud) and the foundational principle of communal supremacy over individual rights.

  • The Supremacy of the Ummah: The community is commanded to "bid what is right and forbid what is wrong" as the "best nation," while those who disagree or resist are labeled as transgressors. Reference: Qur'an 3:110
  • Mandatory Submission: No true faith is recognized until the believer accepts the Prophet as the ultimate judge in all disputes and finds no internal resistance against his decisions. Reference: Qur'an 4:65
  • Physical Penalties: Theft, adultery, and apostasy are met with severe corporal punishments including amputation, flogging, or death. Reference: Qur’an 5:38; 24:2; Hadith Sahih Bukhari 3017, 6810, 6922
  • Moral Policing: Regulation of daily life—dress codes, prayer, and alcohol—often enforced by religious authorities. Reference: Qur'an 5:89

II. Theological and Political Enforcement

Sharia is a totalizing political system that enforces belief and suppresses dissent through legal coercion.

  • No Freedom of Choice: When a matter is decreed by Allah and His Messenger, the believing man or woman has no choice in the matter; disobedience is categorized as going "far astray." Reference: Qur'an 33:36
  • The Prohibition of Alliance: Believers are commanded not to take disbelievers as allies or protectors until they "emigrate for the cause of Allah." Reference: Qur'an 4:89
  • Suppression of Intellectual Freedom: Any criticism of Islamic doctrine or the Prophet is criminalized, strictly limiting freedom of speech and conscience. Reference: Qur'an 33:57 ; Hadith Sunan Abu Dawood 4339
  • Gender and Civil Inequality: The law codifies inequality in inheritance, legal testimony, and personal autonomy. Reference: Qur’an 2:282; 4:11

III. Islamic Jihad and Civilizational Conquest

Scriptural commands provide the mandate for territorial expansion and the legal subjugation of non-believers through capture.

  • Violence in Combat: Specific instructions include striking the "necks" of disbelievers and striking their "every limb joint" during confrontation. Reference: Qur'an 8:12 ; 47:4
  • The Right to Enslave Prisoners: Captives taken during Jihad can be bonded as property (slaves) rather than released. Reference: Qur'an 47:4
  • The Command to Kill Polytheists: Following the sacred months, the command is to capture, besiege, and kill polytheists wherever they are found. Reference: Qur'an 9:5
  • Subjugation of Christians and Jews: War is commanded against the "People of the Book" until they pay the Jizyah (tribute) with willing submission and "feel themselves subdued." Reference: Qur'an 9:29

IV. Historical Evidence and Social Impact

The application of these theological mandates is well-documented throughout history.

  • The Ridda Wars: Military campaigns launched after Muhammad's death to execute those attempting to leave the faith or refuse religious taxes.
  • The Pact of Umar: Historical restrictions that relegated Christians and Jews to Dhimmi (second-class) status, forbidding the building of churches and public prayer.
  • Institutionalized Slavery & Concubinage: The legal codification of enslaving non-Muslims and "those whom your right hands possess." This includes the legal right to sexual relations with female captives and the nullification of their previous marriages upon capture. Reference: Qur'an 23:5-6; 70:29-30 (Concubinage); 4:24 (Captured married women); 24:33 (Treatment/Marriage of slaves)

V. Marriage, Family, and the Law of Guardianship

Sharia law exerts deep control over the private lives of families:

  • Disgusting and Shameless Practices: The legal justification for the mistreatment of women and children, including the allowance of physical abuse and the denial of basic human rights ; which also include [forced] Childs Marriages.
  • Inequality in Marriage: Polygamy is permitted for men, while women have strictly limited rights regarding marriage contracts and the ability to initiate divorce.
  • Male Guardianship Laws: Women are often subject to "Mahram" or guardianship laws, which restrict their freedom of movement, access to education, and personal independence.
  • Economic and Physical Constraint: A woman receives half the inheritance of men and her testimony is worth half. Additionally, men are permitted to physically discipline wives suspected of rebellion. Reference: Qur’an 4:11 (Inheritance); 4:34 (Discipline); 24:6-9 (Accusations); 2:282 (Testimony); 65:4 (Marriage of Minor) ; Hadith Sahih Bukhari 304, 2658 (Woman's Deficiency in mind), 5133 (Muhammad Married Aisha When She was 6 and Consummated the marriage when she was 9)

VI. Conclusion: The Verdict on Autonomy

Life under strict Sharia law imposes a system where personal autonomy, freedom of conscience, and gender equality are legally constrained. These restrictions are not accidental; they are sustained by centuries of codified theological and historical evidence. A system that mandates total submission necessarily invites the extinction of pluralism.

NY ZAVA-MISY MOMBA NY SHARIA: RAFI-PITONDRANA MANAKAIKA TANTERAKA

Raymond Ibrahim--loharano ao amin'ny YouTube: "Raha toa ka ny Islam no tabilao teolojika (blueprint) mamaritra ny finoana, ny Lalàna Sharia/Islamika kosa no milina ara-dalàna tsy maintsy arahina izay mampihatra sy manamafy izany eto amin'ny tontolo hita maso."

I. Ny Lalàna Momba ny Famaizana: Ny Lalàna Mangina ao amin'ny Ummah

Ny Sharia hentitra dia miasa eo ambany famaizana raikitra (Hudud) sy ny foto-kevitry ny fahambonian'ny vondrom-piarahamonina (Ummah) ambonin'ny zon'ny isam-batan'olona.

  • Ny Fahambonian'ny Ummah: Ny fianakaviambe dia voadidy "handidy ny tsara sy handrara ny ratsy" (Amr-bil-Ma'roof wa Nahi-anil-Munkar) amin'ny maha "firenena tsara indrindra" azy, raha toa kosa ka sokajiana ho mpandika lalàna ireo izay manohitra izany. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 3:111
  • Fanekena tsy maintsy atao: Tsy ekena ho manana finoana marina ny olona iray raha tsy manaiky ny Mpaminany ho mpitsara faratampony amin'ny fifandirana rehetra ary tsy mahatsapa fanoherana anaty amin'ny fanapahan-keviny. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 4:66
  • Famaizana ara-batana: Ny halatra, ny fijangajangana, ary ny fialana amin'ny finoana (apostasy) dia faizina amin'ny alalan'ny fanapahana rantsambatana, fikapohana, na famonoana ho faty. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 5:39; 24:3; Hadith Sahih Bukhari 3017, 6810, 6922
  • Moral Policing: Fitsipika mifehy ny fiainana andavanandro — toy ny fitafiana, ny fivavahana, ary ny fandrarana ny toaka. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 5:90

II. Ny Fampiharana ara-Teolojia sy ara-Politika

Ny Sharia dia rafitra politika feno izay manery ny olona hino sy mampitampina ny vavan'izay manohitra amin'ny alalan'ny lalàna.

  • Tsy misy safidy malalaka: Rehefa misy raharaha efa narian'i Allah sy ny Iraky ny Allah, ny lehilahy na vehivavy mpino dia tsy manana safidy intsony; ny tsy fankatoavana dia heverina ho "pifandisoana lehibe." Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 33:37
  • Fandrarana ny Firaisankina: Voarara ny mpino tsy hanao ny tsy mpino ho namana na mpiaro raha tsy hoe "mifindra monina noho ny asan'i Allah" izy ireo. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 4:90
  • Fangejana ny fahalalahana ara-tsaina: Ny fanakianana ny fampianarana Islamika na ny Mpaminany dia heloka bevava. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 33:58 ; Hadith Sunan Abu Dawood 4339
  • Tsy fitoviana eo amin'ny zon'olombelona: Ny lalàna dia manamafy ny tsy fitovian-jo eo amin'ny lova, ny fijoroana ho vavolombelona, ary ny fahaleovantenan'ny tsirairay. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 2:282; 4:12

III. Ny Jihad Islamika sy ny Fanafihana ara-Sivilizasiona

Ny andinin-tsoratra masina dia manome baiko amin'ny fanitaran-tany sy ny fampiasana herisetra amin'ny fakana babo.

  • Herisetra amin'ny ady: Ny toromarika dia ny hikapoka ny "vozona" sy ny "rantsambatana rehetra" an'ireo izay tsy mpino. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 8:13; 47:5
  • Zo hanandevo ny babo an'ady: Ny babo azo amin'ny Jihad dia azo tazonina ho fananana (andevo). Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 47:5
  • Ny baiko hamonoana ny mpanompo sampy: Rehefa tapitra ny volana masina, dia baiko ny hisambotra sy hamono ny mpanompo sampy na aiza na aiza hahitana azy. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 9:6
  • Fampanompoana ny Kristiana sy ny Jiosy: Voadidy ny ady amin'ny "Vahoakan'ny Boky" mandra-pandoany ny Jizyah amin'ny fanekena an-tsitrapo sy fahatsapana ho ambany. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 9:30

IV. Porofo ara-Tantara sy ny fiantraikany ara-Tsosialy

Ny fampiharana an'ireo didy ara-teolojia ireo dia hita mazava tsara mandritra ny tantara.

  • Ny Adin'ny Ridda: Hetsika ara-miaramila hamonoana ireo izay nitady hiala tamin'ny finoana taorian'ny fahafatesan'i Muhammad.
  • Ny Fifanarahan'i Umar (Pact of Umar): Fameperana ara-tantara izay nampiditra ny Kristiana sy ny Jiosy ho ao anatin'ny sokajy Dhimmi (olom-pirenena ambany).
  • Fanandevozana sy fampirafesana amin'ny babo: Ny fampidirana ao anatin'ny lalàna ny fanandevozana ireo tsy mpino sy ny "izay ananan'ny tananao havanana". Tafiditra ao anatin'izany ny zo hanao firaisana ara-nofo amin'ny vehivavy babo sy ny fanafoanana ny fanambadian'izy ireo teo aloha. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 23:6-7; 70:30-31 (Fampirafesana); 4:25 (Vehivavy babo manam-bady); 4:25; 24:34 (Fitondrana andevo)

V. Ny Fanambadiana, ny Fianakaviana, ary ny Lalàna momba ny Mpiahy (Guardianship)

Ny lalàna Sharia dia manana fifehezana lalina amin'ny fiainana manokan'ny fianakaviana:

  • Fomba aman-mpanao maimbo raikina sy tsy misy henatra: Ny fanamarinana ara-dalàna ny fampahoriana ny vehivavy sy ny ankizy, manome lalan-drahalana ny herisetra ara-batana sy ny fandrobana ny zo fototra; izay ahitana koa ny fampanambadiana [an-tery] ny ankizy
  • Tsy fitoviana eo amin'ny Fanambadiana: Ny fampirafesana (polygamy) dia ekena ho an'ny lehilahy, raha voafetra kosa ny zon'ny vehivavy.
  • Ny Lalàn'ny Mpiahy (Male Guardianship): Matetika ny vehivavy no iharan'ny lalàna "Mahram", izay mametra ny fahalalahany hivezivezy sy ny fahaleovantenany.
  • Fangejana ara-Ekonomika sy ara-Batana: Ny vehivavy dia mahazo ny antsasaky ny lovan'ny lehilahy, ary ny fijoroany ho vavolombelona dia mitovy amin'ny antsasaky ny an'ny lehilahy. Ankoatra izany, ny lehilahy dia mahazo mampiasa herisetra ara-batana amin'ny vadiny raha matahotra fanoherana. Andinin-tsoratra: Korany 4:12(Zo ny handova); 4:35 (Famaizana ny vady); 24:7-10 (Fiampangana); 2:283 (Vavolombelona); 65:5 (Fanambadiana ankizy) ;Sahih Bukhari 304, 2658 (Ny tsy fahatomombanan'ny saina)

VI. Fehiny: Ny Didy momba ny Fahaleovantena

Ny fiainana eo ambany Sharia hentitra dia rafitra iray izay mametra tanteraka ny fahaleovantenan'ny isam-batan'olona sy ny fitovian-jo. Ireo fameperana ireo dia nohamafisin'ny porofo ara-teolojia sy ara-tantara efa nisy nandritra ny taonjato maro. Ny rafitra izay manery ny fanekena tanteraka dia mitarika ho amin'ny fahafatesan'ny fahamaroan'ny hevitra (pluralism).
